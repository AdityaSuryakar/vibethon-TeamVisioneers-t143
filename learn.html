What is Artificial Intelligence?

🧠

Core Concept

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines, enabling them to perform tasks that typically require human cognition.

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include:

  • 🗣️ Understanding natural language (NLP)
  • 👁️ Recognizing images and objects (Computer Vision)
  • 🎯 Making decisions and solving problems
  • 🎵 Creating art, music, and text (Generative AI)
  • 🤖 Moving and interacting with the physical world (Robotics)

The AI Hierarchy

Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Broadest field — any technique enabling machines to mimic human behavior
Machine Learning (ML)
Subset of AI — learns from data without being explicitly programmed
Deep Learning (DL)
Subset of ML — uses neural networks with many layers
Generative AI
Creates new content — text, images, code, audio

Types of AI by Capability

🤖

Narrow AI (ANI)

Designed for specific tasks. All current AI systems fall here — chess engines, image recognizers, chatbots.

Current Reality
🧠

General AI (AGI)

Matches human-level intelligence across all domains. Theoretical — does not yet exist.

Future Goal
🌟

Super AI (ASI)

Surpasses human intelligence in all areas. Purely hypothetical and subject of much debate.

Speculative
